🥬 Butterhead & Romaine Lettuce

The perfect, fast-growing crop for hydroponics beginners. Provides crisp leaves in just a few weeks.

📈 Overview & Growing Requirements

Optimal pH Range 5.5 - 6.5
EC Range (Nutrient level) 1.2 - 1.8 mS/cm
Growth Cycle Time 30 - 45 Days
Preferred System NFT or DWC
Water Temperature 18°C - 22°C
Daily Light Requirement 14 - 16 Hours

📋 Step-by-Step Cultivation Guide

1

Germination & Seedling Stage (Days 1–7)

Place lettuce seeds in pre-soaked rockwool cubes or coco coir plugs. Maintain 100% humidity until sprouts emerge, then provide light immediately.

2

Transplanting (Days 10–14)

Once roots protrude from the bottom of the seedling plug, transplant them into DWC rafts or NFT channels. Ensure the root tip contacts the nutrient water.

3

Vegetative Care & Feeding (Days 15–35)

Keep pH locked between 5.5 and 6.5. Monitor EC daily, keeping it at 1.2–1.6. Ensure water flow is continuous in NFT or bubblers are working in DWC.

4

Harvesting (Days 35–45)

Harvest outer leaves progressively for a continuous supply, or cut the entire head at the base once it achieves a firm, full density.
