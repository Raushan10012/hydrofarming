🍅 Vine-Ripened Tomatoes

A highly rewarding, heavy-feeding crop. Perfect for advanced hobbyists using DWC or Dutch Buckets.

📈 Overview & Growing Requirements

Optimal pH Range 6.0 - 6.5
EC Range (Nutrient level) 2.0 - 5.0 mS/cm
Growth Cycle Time 60 - 90 Days
Preferred System DWC or Dutch Buckets
Water Temperature 20°C - 25°C
Daily Light Requirement 16 - 18 Hours

📋 Step-by-Step Cultivation Guide

1

Seed Starter Phase (Days 1–14)

Start seeds in rockwool plugs under high warmth. Keep moist but not saturated. Provide high intensity lighting as soon as seed leaves unfurl.

2

Transplanting into Large Net Cups (Days 15–20)

Transfer seedlings into large DWC net cups filled with expanded clay pebbles (hydroton) or setup inside a Dutch Bucket with coco-perlite mix.

3

Vigorous Growth & Trellising (Days 20–50)

Tomatoes grow tall and heavy. Setup trellises or supports early. Keep EC high (increasing up to 3.0+ mS/cm) and monitor pH to prevent Blossom End Rot.

4

Pollination & Fruiting (Days 50–90)

Inside homes, shake flower clusters gently to hand-pollinate. Once fruits turn deep red, pick them for immediate consumption and rich taste.
