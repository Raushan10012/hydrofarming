🌿 Sweet Genovese Basil

Extremely aromatic and quick-growing herb. Flourishes in NFT or DWC systems and yields leaves in under a month.

📈 Overview & Growing Requirements

Optimal pH Range 5.5 - 6.0
EC Range (Nutrient level) 1.0 - 1.6 mS/cm
Growth Cycle Time 25 - 40 Days
Preferred System NFT or DWC
Water Temperature 18°C - 21°C
Daily Light Requirement 14 - 16 Hours

📋 Step-by-Step Cultivation Guide

1

Sowing & Germination (Days 1–5)

Place 2–3 basil seeds in a rockwool starter plug. Keep wet and warm. Small leafy shoots will sprout within 3 to 5 days.

2

Thinning & Transplanting (Days 6–12)

Thin seedlings to one healthy shoot per plug. Once roots show outside the plug, load it into your NFT channels or DWC raft boards.

3

Pinching & Pruning (Days 12–30)

Pinch the top center stem once it grows 4 sets of leaves. This forces the plant to branch outwards, doubling your yield and avoiding premature flowering.

4

Continuous Harvest (Days 30–40)

Pick leaves from top to bottom. Avoid picking more than 30% of foliage at once. Healthy basil plants will produce new leaves for months!
